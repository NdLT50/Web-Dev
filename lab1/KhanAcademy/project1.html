  1. The Matterhorn

    2. Shaped like a giant pyramid, the Matterhorn is one of the most photographed mountains in the world, making it a top tourist attractions in Switzerland, and that which you need to have on the top of the bucket list on the places to visit in the country. It stands tall at an awe-inspiring 4,478 meters, attracting adrenaline pumped up mountaineers from all over the world to scale the peak. If you’re feeling drained out of energy levels, why not take a cable car instead? The Matterhorn features the highest cable car station in the mountain, which gives you some fabulous viewpoints of the pyramidal peak and beyond.


  2. Château de Chillon

    3. One of Switzerland’s tourist attractions that is difficult to ignore. You’ve no other go than to check out the medieval Château de Chillon. Situated on a small island in Lak Geneva nearby the Montreux town, the Château de Chillon is a 10th-century old caste, initially serving as a tactical war fort. It was later converted into a lakeside summer home for the elite class of Savoy when the rooms of the castle were populated with all types of valuable treasures and artefacts that you’ll be able to enjoy even today.
