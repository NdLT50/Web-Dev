Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Pizza Dough 1 ball
Tomato Sauce 3 tbsp
Fresh Basil 4 leaves

Steps:

1. Roll out the dough on a flat surface.

Pepperoni Pizza

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Mozzarella 1/2 cup
Pepperoni 10 slices
Oregano 1 pinch

Steps:

1. Prepare dough with tomato sauce.

2. Layer mozzarella and pepperoni slices.

3. Sprinkle oregano and bake until crispy.

Veggie Pizza

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Bell Peppers 1/4 cup
Mushrooms 3 large
Black Olives 1 tbsp

Steps:

1. Chop all vegetables into small pieces.

2. Arrange over cheese and sauce base.

3. Bake until vegetables are tender.
